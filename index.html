About Our School

Welcome to the UEIS Family

Established in 2005, Ulaanbaatar Elite International School has become one of Mongolia’s leading international schools with over 850 students from 12 different nationalities.

We offer a dual curriculum that combines the globally recognized Cambridge International Curriculum with the Mongolian National Core Curriculum.
